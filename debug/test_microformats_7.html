Shen Yun offers an exhilarating world-class production that celebrates the grandeur of classical Chinese dance and music. Based in New York, Shen Yun weaves a wondrous tapestry of heavenly realms, enchanting dreams, and marvellous storytelling from ancient heroic legends to epics of the modern day.

A superb cast of the world's foremost artists, masterful choreography, handmade costumes, state-of-the-art backdrops, live orchestral music that brings together instruments spanning east and west.
